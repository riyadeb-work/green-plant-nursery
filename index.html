📦 From Our Nursery To Your Doorstep

Here's how we carefully package, handle, and deliver every plant so it arrives healthy and ready to grow.

1. Order Confirmed

We confirm your order on WhatsApp and select the healthiest plant from our nursery beds.

2. Quality Check

Every plant is inspected for healthy roots, pest-free leaves and sturdy stems.

3. Secure Packaging
